# THE DEATH KNIGHT & THE QUEEN
## FULL SCREENPLAY (CANON)

NOTE:
This screenplay consolidates all scenes written so far into a single continuous script.
Dialogue and action are preserved in cinematic prose format for readability and later conversion
to Final Draft or similar tools.

---

[OPENING – QUEEN ALONE]
Cassandra rules alone. War reports. Necrotic threat implied.

[PRINCESS ERA]
Cassandra meets Joseph. Assigned protector. Trust before romance.
Field scene. Quiet intimacy.
Dragon attack. Joseph reveals death magic.
Grand Healer explains Death Knights.
Cassandra defends Joseph in court.

[DISMISSAL & EXILE]
Joseph dismissed. Silent departure.
Helmet left by Cassandra’s bedside.
Witch-hunt rumors. Cassandra knows it isn’t him.

[STANDOFF I]
Necrotic King appears. Conversion attempt fails.
Joseph draws the Sword of Finality.
Necrotic King: “Finally. A man worth killing.”

[AFTERMATH & SCARS]
Cassandra sees Joseph’s scars.
Learns cost of restraint.

[WAR ESCALATION]
Nations fall. Council realizes scale.

[WEDDING]
Joseph attends unseen. Leaves.
Cassandra searches. Knows.

[RETURN AT THE GATES]
Cassandra has no army.
Joseph’s undead arrive.
Archers turn and fire.
Cavalry charge under Cassandra’s banner.
Joseph collapses.

[TWELVE REVEALED]
Only 11 Death Knights remain.
Feeding truth implied.

[GRAND HEALER]
Joseph past survival threshold.
Truth of the order.

[LOVE CONFESSION]
Cassandra tells Joseph she loves him.

[PALADIN ARC]
Paladin challenges Joseph.
Learns exhaustion warfare.

[MORNING]
Walls rebuilt overnight.
Joseph among the people.

[CONVOCATION]
Surviving nations gather.
Cassandra: “I will bear the cost.”

[STANDOFF II – THRONE ROOM]
Joseph fights on three fronts.
Barrier holds.
Undead dispatched.
Necrotic King reveals godhood.
Joseph reveals he is last Death Knight.
Grand Maester intervenes.
Feeding revealed.

[AFTERMATH]
Guards enter.
Joseph taken to heal.
Council plans without sacrifice.

[GRAND MAESTER CONFESSION]
Joseph was stolen.
Meant to be King Consort.
Feeding by Death Knights.
Guilt revealed.

[CONFRONTATION]
Cassandra confronts Death Knights.
Feeding stopped.

[RESTRAINT BREAK]
Joseph confronts Grand Maester.
Almost loses restraint.

[ANCHOR]
Cassandra stabilizes Joseph.

[CONFESSION]
Joseph admits years of blame, secrecy, manipulation.

[KING CONSORT]
Private confrontation.
Truth acknowledged.
