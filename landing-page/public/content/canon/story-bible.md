# THE DEATH KNIGHT & THE QUEEN
## STORY BIBLE (CANON)

- Joseph: Last living Death Knight, finite, restrained, mortal.
- Cassandra: Queen, political and emotional anchor.
- Necrotic King: Death-aligned god, attrition and dominion.
- Death Knights: Extinct order, sacrifice-based deterrents.
- Core Theme: Restraint vs Dominion; Love vs Oblivion; Sacrifice with Choice.
