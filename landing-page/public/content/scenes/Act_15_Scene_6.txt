FADE IN:

EXT. NARROW VALLEY - NIGHT

The Necrotic King offers his hand.

                              NECROTIC KING
                  Join me. End the suffering.

                              JOSEPH
                  You end choice, not suffering.

                              NECROTIC KING
                  Choice is a wound.

                              JOSEPH
                  Then I will keep it open.

He refuses.

FADE OUT.
