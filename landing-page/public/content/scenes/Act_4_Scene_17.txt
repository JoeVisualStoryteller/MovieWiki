FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE CORRIDOR - DAY

Cassandra walks with the GRAND MAESTER. Their footsteps echo.

                              CASSANDRA
                  There is a limit.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  There always was.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then I must decide where it lies.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  And live with the cost.

She nods, already living with it.

FADE OUT.
