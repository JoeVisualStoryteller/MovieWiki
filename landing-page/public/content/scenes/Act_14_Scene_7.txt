FADE IN:

EXT. NARROW VALLEY - DAY

The valley is a kill box. Undead masses close in. The living dig in.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Hold the choke. We take the head.

                              GENERAL TESSA
                  And if the head does not appear?

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we cut until it does.

Her command is calm, deadly.

FADE OUT.
