FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - WAR ROOM - DAY

A map marker for the northern capital is removed. The sound of the
pin is too loud.

                              COUNCILOR RAIM
                  The population is converting.

                              GENERAL TESSA
                  We have no buffer.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then Astraea is the buffer.

She says it like a sentence, not a plea.

FADE OUT.
