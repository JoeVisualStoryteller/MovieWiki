FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE STUDY - NIGHT

Cassandra absorbs the confession. The room feels smaller.

                              CASSANDRA
                  The living did this.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  Yes. We killed the restraint we needed.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we are living inside our own crime.

The Grand Maester nods, unable to deny it.

FADE OUT.
