INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS

Joseph touches her cheek, then pulls away.

                              JOSEPH
                  You taught me restraint.

                              CASSANDRA
                  You taught me the cost of it.

                              JOSEPH
                  And you still choose it.

                              CASSANDRA
                  I choose you through it.

They part without a kiss.

FADE OUT.
