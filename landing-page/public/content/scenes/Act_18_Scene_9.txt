FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE STUDY - DAY

The GRAND MAESTER speaks quietly.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  The Necrotic King bound his dominion to a source.
                  Until it is cut, the dead will rise.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we cut it.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  That will draw him back.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we cut him again.

FADE OUT.
