FADE IN:

EXT. ASTRAEA - TRAINING YARD - NIGHT

Cassandra watches Joseph drill a mixed unit. She sees the cost in his
posture.

                              CASSANDRA
                  You should rest.

                              JOSEPH
                  Rest is a luxury we do not have.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we must manufacture it.

He glances at her, a flicker of appreciation.

FADE OUT.
