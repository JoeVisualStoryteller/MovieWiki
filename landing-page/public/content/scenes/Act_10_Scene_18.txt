FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE CHAMBER - NIGHT

Cassandra receives a warning from a trusted aide.

                              AIDE
                  The court plots to expose him.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then the court learns restraint the hard way.

                              AIDE
                  That will make enemies.

                              CASSANDRA
                  The dead are enemies. The living are undecided.

She dismisses the aide, resolved.

FADE OUT.
