FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE ANTECHAMBER - DAY

Joseph watches the envoy from behind a screen. He is invisible to all
but Cassandra.

                              JOSEPH
                  They will want proof.

                              CASSANDRA
                  They will get restraint, not spectacle.

                              JOSEPH
                  That will test them.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then let it.

He studies her, approving.

FADE OUT.
