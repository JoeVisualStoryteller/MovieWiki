FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - PRIVATE CHAMBER - DAY

Joseph enters, pale.

                              JOSEPH
                  A direct strike will cost me everything.

                              CASSANDRA
                  And if we do nothing, it costs everyone.

                              JOSEPH
                  Then I will pay it.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Only if you choose it.

He nods, consent clear.

FADE OUT.
