FADE IN:

INT. HEALING TENT - DAY

The GRAND MAESTER warns her.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  If we pull him back, it will spend him.

                              CASSANDRA
                  He deserves to choose.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  And you deserve to live.

                              CASSANDRA
                  That was never promised.

He nods.

FADE OUT.
