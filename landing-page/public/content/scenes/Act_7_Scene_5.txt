FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - COUNCIL CHAMBER - MIDDAY

Ancient records are laid out. Prohibitions written by hands long dust.

The Grand Maester traces one line.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  Every public recall ended the same way.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Better?

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  Faster.

Cassandra absorbs that.

                              CASSANDRA
                  And a private one?

The Grand Maester meets her gaze.

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  That has never been attempted.

FADE OUT.
