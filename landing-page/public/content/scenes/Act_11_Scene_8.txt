FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - FIELD INFIRMARY - DAY

Cassandra walks among the injured. A medic wipes blood from his hands.

                              MEDIC
                  They turned while alive.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we end him before he can do it again.

                              MEDIC
                  And if we cannot?

                              CASSANDRA
                  Then we die refusing him.

FADE OUT.
