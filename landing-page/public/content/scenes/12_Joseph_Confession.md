## SCENE — What I Carried Alone
INT. QUIET ANTECHAMBER – NIGHT

Joseph confesses years of blame.

JOSEPH
They thought I was the monster.

He admits sneaking back to be fed, letting the world hate him.

JOSEPH (CONT’D)
Seeing you and not speaking was the hardest part.

Cassandra takes his hand.

CASSANDRA
You don’t disappear anymore.
