FADE IN:

INT. ASTRAEA - HEALING CRYPT ANTECHAMBER - NIGHT

The GRAND MAESTER exits. Cassandra stands.

                              CASSANDRA
                  How long?

                              GRAND MAESTER
                  Until he chooses to wake.

                              CASSANDRA
                  Or until his body chooses for him.

The Grand Maester does not deny it.

FADE OUT.
