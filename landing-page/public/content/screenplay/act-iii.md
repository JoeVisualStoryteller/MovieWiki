THE DEATH KNIGHT & THE QUEEN
ACT III — CONSEQUENCE AND CHOICE

---

SCENE 16 — THE FIRST SIN
INT. GRAND MAESTER’S CHAMBER – NIGHT

Joseph lies unconscious on the stone table.
Bandages pulse faintly, then dim.

Cassandra stands beside him.

GRAND MAESTER
He should have died tonight.

CASSANDRA
But he didn’t.

GRAND MAESTER
Because he wasn’t alone.

The Grand Maester confesses.

He was the first Death Knight.
He stole Joseph as a child.
Joseph was meant to be Cassandra’s King Consort.

GRAND MAESTER
I ran.

He admits the Eleven fed Joseph with their own existence.

Cassandra understands the cruelty.

CASSANDRA
We are ending this.

---

SCENE 17 — THE DEBT IS NAMED
INT. INNER CRYPT – NIGHT

Cassandra confronts the Eleven Death Knights.

CASSANDRA
You’ve been feeding him.

They do not deny it.

She orders them to stop.

CASSANDRA
No one dies quietly for him again.

They kneel.

---

SCENE 18 — IF I LET GO
INT. CASTLE CORRIDOR – NIGHT

Joseph awakens.
He heard everything.

He confronts the Grand Maester.

Death magic surges.
Torches extinguish.
Stone cracks.

JOSEPH
This is what happens without restraint.

He nearly erases the Grand Maester.

Joseph pulls back.

---

SCENE 19 — THE ANCHOR
INT. CORRIDOR – NIGHT

Cassandra senses the disturbance.

She finds Joseph on the edge.

She grounds him.

CASSANDRA
You came back.

JOSEPH
Barely.

Restraint reasserts.

---

SCENE 20 — WHAT I CARRIED ALONE
INT. QUIET ANTECHAMBER – NIGHT

Joseph confesses years of blame.

He snuck back to be fed.
Let the world hate him.
Saw Cassandra but could not speak.

JOSEPH
I stood at your wedding.

Cassandra takes his hand.

CASSANDRA
You don’t disappear anymore.

---

SCENE 21 — BEFORE THE DOOR CLOSES
INT. ROYAL APARTMENTS – NIGHT

The King Consort confronts Cassandra.

KING CONSORT
You love him.

CASSANDRA
Yes.

He accepts it with dignity.

KING CONSORT
I’m making space.

---

SCENE 22 — BY MORNING
EXT. CITY – DAWN

The walls are rebuilt overnight.

Undead stand guard.

Joseph sits among the people.
They bring him food.

JOSEPH
You told me I was forbidden from leaving.

CASSANDRA
So you stayed.

---

SCENE 23 — THOSE WHO STILL STAND
INT. GREAT HALL – DAY

Surviving nations convene.

CASSANDRA
You should be thankful.
I will bear the cost.

Joseph’s life.

The room falls silent.

---

END ACT III
